Katie

As a California native, Katie has brought a special energy and vibrancy to the salon during her five years as a stylist. Blondes and balayage are her business, and she loves to pursue every chance to continually sharpen her skills. Katie has traveled to NYC several times for cutting and coloring classes while also learning from the many artists that have taught at ViVo. A lover of the outdoors, she spends free time at the lake or by the pool with friends and family.
